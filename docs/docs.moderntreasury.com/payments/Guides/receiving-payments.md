# Receiving Payments

After onboarding an [Individual](onboard-an-individual) or [Business](onboard-a-business) Legal Entity, and opening an Internal Account for that Legal Entity, you now have a routable account where you can receive payments from other bank accounts or stablecoin wallets.

For example, this could be because you are receiving an RTP (money pushed to your account), receiving an ACH debit (money pulled from an external account), or receiving a stablecoin transfer from an externally managed stablecoin wallet.

This guide will walk you through how to identify your Internal Account details, track payments coming into that Internal Account and perform any actions you may require after the payment is received.
