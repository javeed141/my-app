# Mark as Resolved

Upon notification from your bank that your Payment Order hold is resolved, you have the ability to manually resolve the hold directly in the Modern Treasury Dashboard.

<Callout icon="ℹ️" theme="info">
  Resolving a hold in Modern Treasury has no impact on the processing of the payment.

  If your bank communicates to Modern Treasury an update of your held Payment Order's status, it will be automatically marked as resolved and updated.
</Callout>

# How to Mark your Payment Order as Resolved

1. Login to Modern Treasury
2. Navigate to the [Payments Overview](https://app.moderntreasury.com/payment_orders) page
3. Locate the Payment Order subject of the hold, open it's details page
4. Open the **Actions** dropdown in top right
5. Select **Resolve Hold**

   <Image align="left" border={false} width="150px" src="https://files.readme.io/a78fb49c6896d74e67d5ce4df129892719efed868434753cf6673d5fee292b7f-Screenshot_2025-10-09_at_12.00.39.png" />
6. Review Payment Order details
7. Specify a **Resolution** for the hold (optional)
8. Confirm **Mark as Resolved**

The Payment Order status will be updated to its last known status prior to being flagged as held.
