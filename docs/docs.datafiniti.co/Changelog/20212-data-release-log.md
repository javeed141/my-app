# 2021.2 - Data Release Log

# Overview

This month saw a dramatic impact on our property database.  We were required to remove a significant portion due to specific sources making changes.  Going forward we are looking at additional sources to supplement this loss.

## Record Counts

The following are the updated record counts for each vertical:

* Business data decreased by 1.2 million to 118.2 million
* People data stayed the same at 3 million
* Product data increased by 2.7 million to 265.5 million
* Property data decreased by 49.7 million to 117 million

## Fixes

* Updated 2 Business data sources, 2 Product data sources, and 3 Property data sources
