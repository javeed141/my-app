# Modifying Dispute Configurations

Understand how to change your dispute configurations.

Your dispute configuration controls important behaviors like automatic provisional credit issuance, write-off thresholds, decision engine settings, and communication templates.

**To modify any dispute configuration:**

1. Email your request and reason for change to **[enterprise.disputes@lithic.com](mailto:enterprise.disputes@lithic.com)**
2. A Lithic agent will confirm receipt within **3 business days**
3. Configuration changes typically take **30 days**, though timeline may vary based on complexity

**Common configuration changes:**

* Decision thresholds (auto provisional credit amounts, write-off limits)
* Regulation obligation settings (Reg E vs Reg Z handling)
* Communication templates (signature, logo, messaging)
* Automatic recovery attempt settings
* Manual review triggers
* Cardholder communication preferences (Lithic-managed vs self-managed)
