# Export a Report

How to export a Report as a CSV

Occasionally, you may find it helpful to obtain a report for a presentation or for integrating to an Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system. This guide outlines the steps you need to take to export a report as a CSV.

> 🚧 Reporting Timezones
>
> Within the Modern Treasury App, your reporting will reflect in local time, but all CSV exports will reflect in Coordinated Universal Time (UTC).
>
> Manual Ledger CSV exports will reflect your configured timezone.

## To export a Report

1. Log into Modern Treasury
2. Navigate to the [Reporting](https://app.moderntreasury.com/reporting/reports) section from the left sidebar
3. Select the report you wish to export
4. Click **Export CSV**, and a notification should appear at the top of the page stating *"Your CSV export is being processed and will arrive in your email shortly"*
5. Within a few minutes, you will receive an email from Modern Treasury where you can download the export to your computer

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Do exports expire?**

A report export will be available for download indefinitely.

**Are the exports dynamic?**

The data included in the download link will remain static from the time the export was created.
