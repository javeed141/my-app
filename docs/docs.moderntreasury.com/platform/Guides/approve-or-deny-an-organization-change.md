# Approving or Denying an Organization Change

How to approve organization changes made by other administrators

> 🚧 Organization Change Approvals Required
>
> In order to use this feature, you must have [Organization Change Approvals](https://docs.moderntreasury.com/platform/docs/organization-change-approvals) enabled. You can do so by ensuring you meet the [prerequisites listed here](https://docs.moderntreasury.com/platform/docs/organization-change-approvals) and follow the instructions to enable the feature.

# 1. Go to the change requiring approval

Navigate to Settings > Approval Rules. Click on the "Admin Approvals" tab. Select the change you wish to review.

# 2. Review the change

You will be able to review differences before and after the change.

Given approvals are "all or nothing," be sure to review the changes in full. You cannot accept partial changes (e.g. one condition to a rule but not another).

If you proposed the change, you cannot review it.

<Image align="center" className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/aaf3dca4d7a657eb68685d96ab9719005fa4a9b7d475a81801f4ad483e8af84c-Screenshot_2025-02-18_at_3.43.35_PM.png" />

# 3. Approve or deny the change

Once you have reviewed the change, click to approve or deny.

If you approve the change, it will immediately be applied. If you deny the change, no changes will be made to the object.

For proposed creates, you will be redirected back to the admin approvals pending changes list.

For proposed updates, you will stay on the updated object's details view.
