# 2025.10 - Data Release Log

# Summary

We’re excited to bring you some new updates focused on enhancing transparency, structure, and usability. Our commitment to providing you with the best possible experience drives these changes, and we believe you'll find them to be impactful to your workflow.

# Property Data

## New Counties Source Added

We have added several county sources to enhance your property data searches. We've significantly expanded our property data sources by integrating new county databases. This enhances our coverage with richer datasets, including property records, tax assessments, and ownership history, providing you with more comprehensive and accurate information for efficient, informed decision-making.

  * Sonoma County

  * Camden County

# Portal

## County Autocomplete

We've added autocomplete functionality to every county text box within the Datafiniti portal. This enhancement streamlines your data entry process, making it faster and more accurate by suggesting relevant county names as you type. You'll experience reduced typing errors and improved efficiency in your workflows.

# Coming soon

## Business Data & Property Data Search

Just like how we created the autotrace feature for cross referencing people and property data, we are doing the same for business and property data. Our goal is to cross reference and business with all the business data schema fields to the equivalent property record.

## Product Data - New Pricing Field

We are working on adding a new pricing field dedicated to the first price seen on products within Datafiniti’s dataset. This new pricing field will allow users to easily track and analyze the initial price of products as first observed by Datafiniti, providing a clear reference point for price changes and market analysis.
