## Subscription

Subscription plans can be updated per data type. In the setting page, you can select the subscription tab to view your current subscription plans and credits available to your current billing period.

<https://portal.datafiniti.co/settings/billing/subscription>

> 🚧 Changing subscription plans
>
> Please note that changing your subscription plan will reset your credits available and payment period due date to the confirmed update time. Changing plans does not roll over unused credits that were available before updating your plan.
