## Overview

### Payment Plans

#### Annual vs. Monthly Billing

Choose what suits you best: our more flexible monthly billing or the annual option that comes with a neat discount. It's all about giving you options that fit your project's timeline and budget.

#### ACH Payments

For those who prefer a streamlined approach to payments, we offer the option for ACH payments.

### Billing Security with Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

Security is a top priority, and we accommodate cards that require Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA). This means that every transaction is double-checked and verified by the issuing bank, adding an extra layer of security to comply with local regulations.

## The Open Source Plan: Collaboration and Innovation

We :heart: Open Source! Our commitment to the community extends beyond our own products. We actively contribute to and maintain several open source projects that you can use to enhance your own documentation and development processes.

Our Open Source Plan is designed to support and celebrate the open source community. It offers tools and resources to help your open source projects thrive.

### Eligibility

To qualify for the Open Source Plan, your project must:

* Be hosted on GitHub or Bitbucket
* Be entirely open source (not just an SDK or library related to a closed source project)

### Features

The Open Source Plan includes all the features of the Startup plan, except:

* **Custom Domains:** Disabled; projects can only be accessed via their ReadMe subdomain (e.g., opensource.readme.io).
* **Access Control:** The project’s access is locked to Public. (Access to Project Admins Only and Site-wide Password cannot be selected.)

### How to Apply

To apply for the Open Source Plan:

1. Go to your **Project Dashboard**.
2. Navigate to **Configuration > Manage Plan**.
3. Click on the **Open Source** application link and fill out the form.

Our team will review your information and grant access as soon as possible.

### Join the Open Source Community

The Open Source Plan is more than just access to features; it’s an opportunity to innovate and collaborate within the open source community. Whether you’re an experienced developer or new to open source, this plan can help you achieve your goals.
