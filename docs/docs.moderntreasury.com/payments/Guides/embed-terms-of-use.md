# Embed Terms of Use

### Requirement

You are required to ensure that your end users agree to Modern Treasury’s Terms of Use in a legally enforceable manner, including any regulatory and compliance requirements specific to your business (e.g. E-sign consents if your end users are consumers). This ensures your end users are informed of, and agree to, the terms governing MT-powered payment functionality.

End users must agree to both your legal terms as well as the Modern Treasury Terms of Use, before accessing MT Payments. This acceptance should occur prior to enabling any MT-powered payment accounts or functionality.

### How to embed Modern Treasury's Terms of Use

You may embed Modern Treasury’s Terms of Use into your existing legal terms rather than presenting a separate agreement if you prefer. The most common approach is to include a term in your own legal terms that states:

> \[end customer] agrees to the Modern Treasury Terms of Use available at [https://www.moderntreasury.com/legal/terms-of-use](https://www.moderntreasury.com/legal/terms-of-use)

Please consult your lawyers to ensure that this approach comports with your own legal and compliance posture and is legally enforceable under your own legal terms.

Your legal terms should clearly state that certain payment features are powered by Modern Treasury and subject to its Terms of Use.

The current version of Modern Treasury’s Terms of Use is available [here](https://www.moderntreasury.com/legal/terms-of-use).

### Ongoing compliance

You are responsible for ensuring your legal terms remain up to date and continue to bind end-users to the current version of Modern Treasury’s Terms of Use.

### Exemption for Bring Your Own Bank Customers

If you use Modern Treasury solely for [direct bank integrations](https://docs.moderntreasury.com/payments/docs/internal-accounts-overview) you are not required to share Modern Treasury's Terms of Use with your end users.
