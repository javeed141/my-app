# Managing your bank integrations

Modern Treasury supports bringing your own bank to use for payments. In most cases, integrating your bank account will require setup and coordination with Modern Treasury and your partner bank. The bank accounts must be opened and ready for direct transmission before we can start the implementation.

Please reach out to your CSM or [support@moderntreasury.com](mailto:support@moderntreasury.com) to kickstart the process with our Bank Integrations Team.
